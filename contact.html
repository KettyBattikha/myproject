Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership options?

We offer flexible membership plans including monthly, quarterly, and annual options. All memberships include access to our full range of equipment, group classes, and basic amenities. Premium memberships also include personal training sessions and additional perks.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions, small group training, and specialized programs. You can book sessions through our app or speak with our front desk staff.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our priority. We maintain rigorous cleaning protocols, have trained staff on-site during all operating hours, provide safety equipment, and conduct regular equipment maintenance. We also offer safety orientations for new members.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes, we offer flexible options for freezing or canceling memberships. Freezing is available for medical reasons, travel, or other circumstances. Cancellation policies vary by membership type, and we're happy to discuss your options.

Do you have parking available?

We provide free parking for all members and visitors. Our parking lot has over 100 spaces and is well-lit for evening workouts. We also have bike racks for those who prefer to cycle to the gym.
